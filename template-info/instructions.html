How to hide the Flex Hero block from the home page?

Highlight the "Flex Hero" block and go to the Style panel.
Click Display: None under Layout

How to display the Flex Hero block from the home page?

Highlight the "Flex Hero" block and go to the Style panel.
Click Display: Flex under Layout
